Welcome to Our Feedback System

Our feedback system is designed to facilitate communication between students and staff, allowing for the submission of feedback, suggestions, and reports on various aspects of campus life.

Learn more

Benefits

For Students

  • Improves communication between students and staff.
  • Allows students to submit feedback, suggestions, and reports.
  • Helps in resolving issues efficiently.
  • Enhances the overall campus experience.

For Staff

  • Facilitates receiving and managing feedback from students.
  • Enables efficient response to student queries and concerns.
  • Provides insights for continuous improvement of campus services.

Instructions

To use the feedback system, follow these instructions:

  1. Sign in to your account using your credentials.
  2. Click on the "Submit Feedback" button to provide your feedback.
  3. You can track the status of your feedback in the "Feedback Status" section.
  4. If you need assistance, you can contact support through the "Support" page.
